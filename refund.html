Claims & Refunds

We strive to provide accurate vehicle history reports. If you're not satisfied with our service, we're here to help with our straightforward claims and refund process.

Claims Process

1

Submit Your Claim

Fill out our claim form with your order details and the reason for your claim. Include your VIN number and purchase date.

2

Review Process

Our team will review your claim within 24-48 hours and verify the information provided.

3

Resolution

Once approved, we'll process your claim and notify you of the outcome via email.

Refund Policy

Eligibility Criteria

  • Request within 30 days of purchase
  • Inaccurate or incomplete report data
  • Technical issues preventing report access

Refund Timeline

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval. The amount will be credited to your original payment method.

Non-Refundable Cases

  • Expired refund window (>30 days)
  • Multiple report downloads
